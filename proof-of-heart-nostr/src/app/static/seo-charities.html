Bitcoin Charities & Non-Profits That Accept Bitcoin

Proof of Heart is an open bitcoin charity platform. It helps bitcoin charities and non-profits publish transparent donation profiles so supporters can donate bitcoin or lightning directly to wallets the organization controls.

Browse all listed bitcoin charities or list your own bitcoin non-profit.

What Is a Bitcoin Charity Donation Endpoint?

A bitcoin charity donation endpoint is a single, reusable destination that donors can trust:

  • An on-chain bitcoin address and QR code for donations.
  • Optionally, a lightning endpoint for fast, low-fee payments.
  • Public information about the charity and how funds are used.

Instead of random addresses scattered across the internet, an endpoint gives donors one canonical place to donate bitcoin and verify the non-profit’s details.

Why Openness Matters for Bitcoin Charities

Bitcoin donors want to be confident that their support is going to the right organization. An open profile on Proof of Heart helps by:

  • Publishing clear information: name, mission, links, and donation details.
  • Reducing impersonation risk: donors can compare the profile with the charity’s own website.
  • Enabling community review: anyone can inspect endpoints and evaluate the organization.

Your endpoint becomes the reference point for donors who want to support your bitcoin charity safely.

Direct Bitcoin Donations and Self-Custody

When your organization is listed on Proof of Heart, donations do not pass through a custodial platform. Instead:

  • The charity controls its own keys and funds.
  • Donors see exactly where they send bitcoin or lightning payments.
  • There is no platform balance to withdraw from or lose access to.

This self-custody model treats bitcoin non-profits as full Bitcoin users, not just beneficiaries of payouts.

How to List Your Bitcoin Non-Profit on Proof of Heart

  1. Go to the onboarding page.
  2. Connect your charity signer and create your profile.
  3. Set up your bitcoin and lightning donation endpoints.
  4. Publish your profile so donors can discover you.

Once your profile is live, link to it from your website’s “Donate” page and share it with your bitcoin community.
