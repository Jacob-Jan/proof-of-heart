About us

The information age is rapidly taking shape. More and more people decide to adopt Bitcoin as the only sound money currently available in cyberspace (Glassnode). At the time of writing this blogpost, nearly all charities are still using government controlled money (fiat). We believe that our planet will greatly benefit from a decentralized and sustainable form of money (Ammous).

Why

Our mission is to help charities make the switch to the Bitcoin standard and provide the Bitcoin community with options to donate to charities who align with their vision.

How

Our vision is twofold: create a donation platform where the donor is assured that bitcoin will be sent directly to the charity wallet, and onboard charities to self-custody.

Who

We are Maurits and Jacob, Bitcoin maximalists from the Netherlands.
