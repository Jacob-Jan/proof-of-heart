Bitcoin Donations - Direct Giving to Self-Custody Charities

Bitcoin donations let you support causes you care about using open, borderless money. On Proof of Heart you can donate bitcoin or donate lightning directly to self-custody charities, so your contribution flows straight from your wallet to theirs.

Browse bitcoin charities or add your charity to the register.

How Bitcoin Donations Work

A bitcoin donation is a transaction on the Bitcoin network from your address to a charity’s address. In practice, the process is simple:

  1. Find a charity that accepts bitcoin on Proof of Heart.
  2. Open its official bitcoin donation profile.
  3. Send funds from your wallet to the on-chain address or lightning invoice.

The transaction is recorded on the blockchain (or via lightning), and the charity can manage the funds directly in its own wallet.

Donate Bitcoin vs Donate Lightning

Many charities listed on Proof of Heart can receive both on-chain bitcoin donations and lightning donations:

  • On-chain bitcoin is ideal for larger or less frequent gifts.
  • Lightning donations are perfect for small, instant contributions.

Proof of Heart treats both as part of a single donation endpoint. You choose how to donate bitcoin based on your wallet, fees, and the urgency of your support.

Why Self-Custody Matters for Bitcoin Donations

A self-custody charity holds its own keys instead of relying on an exchange or custodial payment processor. This matters because:

  • Funds cannot be frozen by a third-party custodian.
  • The organization designs its own security and treasury policy.
  • There is a direct relationship between donor and recipient on the Bitcoin network.

When you donate bitcoin on Proof of Heart, you support charities that treat bitcoin as a native asset, held in wallets they control.

How to Make Your First Bitcoin Donation

  1. Visit the Proof of Heart donate page.
  2. Choose a charity whose mission you believe in.
  3. Open its profile and verify the bitcoin donation endpoint.
  4. Scan the QR code or paste the address from your wallet and send.

In just a few confirmations, your bitcoin donation arrives directly in the charity’s wallet, ready to be used for real-world impact.
