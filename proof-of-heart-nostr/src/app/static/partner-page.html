Partner with Proof of Heart

If you run a bitcoin business, wallet, meetup group, podcast or online community, you can help more people donate bitcoin to real-world charities by pointing interested users to the Proof of Heart register.
Doing so also signals that your project supports self-custody bitcoin charity infrastructure.

On this page you'll find simple HTML snippets you can embed on your website. They show a small “Donate bitcoin to charities” badge with our logo and link directly to https://proofofheart.org.

Who this is for

This embed is designed for:

  • Bitcoin wallets and exchanges with users asking how to donate bitcoin.
  • Bitcoin educators, newsletters, and content creators.
  • Communities, meetups, and NGOs that want to highlight bitcoin donations.

It is intentionally low-key: only visitors who are already curious about bitcoin donations will click through.

How this helps

  • People who care about bitcoin donations get a clear, trusted destination for their giving.
  • Charities listed on Proof of Heart get more visibility and direct support, without intermediaries.
  • You show your users that you support non-custodial, self-custody bitcoin charity infrastructure.
