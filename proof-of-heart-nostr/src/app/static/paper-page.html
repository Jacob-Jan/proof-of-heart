Proof of Heart

Nostr-native charity discovery and direct Bitcoin giving

Version 2.0 — February 2026

Abstract

Proof of Heart is a Nostr-native charity platform. Charities connect with a Nostr signer, publish profile data as events, and receive donations directly through their own lightning/zap endpoints. The platform indexes public data, improves discovery, and applies transparent community moderation. No custody. No private key collection. Don’t trust, verify.

1. Platform principles

  • Charity identity is anchored to a public key (npub).
  • Profile metadata is portable and relay-based.
  • Community signals (followers, reports, ratings) are open and auditable.
  • Donations are direct, non-custodial, and charity-controlled.

2. Product model

  • Nostr connect for charities: sign in with a NIP-07 signer (extension/connector), never share private key.
  • Base profile from Nostr metadata: re-use standard profile data immediately.
  • Proof of Heart extension fields: publish app-specific charity fields via custom kind events.
  • Direct giving: donors donate via lightning/zaps to charity-controlled endpoints.
  • Open reputation: follower counts, ratings, and reports are visible and explainable.

3. Event model

  • kind 0: standard Nostr profile metadata (name, picture, about, lud16/lud06, etc.).
  • kind 30078: Proof of Heart charity profile extension (mission, country, category, donation message, optional lightning override).
  • kind 30079: Proof of Heart charity rating events.
  • kind 1984 (NIP-56): reports/flags for moderation signals.
  • kind 3: follow graph used for social proof (follower count estimation).

4. Moderation policy (transparent + tunable)

Moderation is based on public events and deterministic rules, not hidden admin actions.

  • Reports are counted by unique reporter pubkeys.
  • Charities can be soft-hidden when report threshold is reached.
  • Thresholds are configurable (e.g., 1 for strict mode, 3 for balanced mode).
  • Hidden status is explainable in UI ("hidden due to X flags").

5. Donations and trust model

  • Proof of Heart does not custody funds.
  • Donors pay charity endpoints directly.
  • Trust comes from open identity, cross-checkable metadata, and public social signals.
  • Platform value is discovery, indexing, and safer UX—not financial intermediation.

6. Roadmap

  • Improve relay coverage strategy
  • Add insight in raters
  • Add insight in flaggers

Conclusion

Proof of Heart provides open identities, open reputation, and direct Bitcoin donations for charities. The goal is to help donors support legitimate charities with minimal trust and maximal transparency.
