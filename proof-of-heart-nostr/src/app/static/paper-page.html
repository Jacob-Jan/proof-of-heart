Proof of Heart

Direct, non-custodial giving for real-world charities

Version 1.0 — August 2025

Abstract

Proof of Heart is a free service, providing a web application that lets any charity publish a profile with bitcoin donation endpoints, enabling donors to give without intermediaries. We will provide donors with easy options to verify themselves whether a charity profile is legitimate. Don’t trust, verify.

1. Purpose

Charitable giving is too often mediated by platforms that custody funds, harvest data, throttle access, or de-platform at will. Donors lose privacy; charities lose sovereignty; both pay rent to gatekeepers.

The internet made giving global, Bitcoin fixes the money. Proof of Heart fixes discovery and trust: a clear, public way to find and verify charities and pay them directly.

2. Product

  • Public charity profiles
  • Direct donation endpoints: charity-controlled BTCPay Server
  • Options to verify charity profiles
  • Design principles: directness over intermediaries; verifiability over virality; privacy by default; no tokens, no rent-seeking.

3. Architecture & Flow

Roles

  • Charity: controls its own receiving infrastructure.
  • Donor: chooses (and verifies) a charity and pays at the charity’s endpoint.
  • Register (Proof of Heart): lists charities, verifies simple proofs, and helps donors navigate safely.

4. Roadmap

Live

  • Register + profiles
  • Direct BTCPay links

Upcoming

  • Filter charities on cause, region, proof, etc.
  • Charity profile verification proofs.
  • Community signal: rate, review, and flag charities

5. Conclusion

Proof of Heart is deliberately narrow, a clean register with simple proofs and a clear path to find, verify and donate bitcoin to charities. No custody, no intermediaries. Just donors, charities and Bitcoin doing what it already does best.
